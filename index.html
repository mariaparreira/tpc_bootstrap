Golden Retriever Puppy




Caring for your puppy:

Once you get a puppy you should go straight to the vet, to get a checkup. Don't be afraid to ask questions.

Buy quality food for your puppy. Why not, ey? He deserves it, give him the best care you can.

Teach him where he should pee and poo. Take him out regularly, so he doesn't pee at home.

Buy some toys and a bed.

Give him love! Love him like it's the last day on Earth.
